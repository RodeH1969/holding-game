Rules

Points are earned by a player holding the ball in each quarter at exactly 10 minutes on the clock, as judged and communicated by the Games Master.

If no player is holding the ball at those moments then the next player to hold the ball earns the points except where 30 seconds passes after the designated time wherein no player earns points.

A tap is not holding
A kick is not holding
2 hands of the player on the ball IS holding
In all cases the ruling of the Games Master is FINAL

SCORING SYSTEM

Points scale up every single quarter: Q1 scores 1 Pt, Q2 scores 2 Pts, Q3 scores 3 Pts, Q4 scores 4 Pts!

NOT HOLDING! ❌

Kicking or foot contact does NOT count. Zero points. Only hands-on catches qualify.

NOT HOLDING! ❌

A single-handed punch, spoil, or tap does NOT qualify. The ball must be fully gripped.

THIS IS HOLDING! 🏈✨

Exactly at 10:00 (or first touch before 10:30), a player must physically secure the Sherrin with 2 hands!
