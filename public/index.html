Rules

Points are earned by a player holding the ball in each quarter at exactly the 15 minute mark as judged and communicated by the Games Master.

If no player is holding the ball at those moments then the next player to hold the ball earns the points except where 30 seconds passes after the designated time wherein no player earns points.

A tap is not holding
A kick is not holding
2 hands of the player on the ball IS holding
In all cases the ruling of the Games Master is FINAL

How to Claim Prizes

The Games Master will post the official results after each quarter in the Leaderboard tab.

To claim Prize 1: The player who is leading at the 15:00 mark of the 4th quarter must text the Games Master before the final siren. The text message must say:
"LEADER"

To claim Prize 2: Text the Games Master before the final siren of the 4th quarter. The text message must say:
"WINNER"
