How to Claim Prizes

Prize 1
You must be the LEADER on Total Points after all 4 quarters have been decided. If you're leading, text "LEADER" to Game Master 0459 754 708 before the final siren.

Prize 2
ALL 4 of your players must have held the ball at some point across the 4 quarters. If they have, text "WINNER" to Game Master 0459 754 708 before the final siren.
